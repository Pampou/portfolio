About

Developer with more than 6 years of professional experience on Unity, I am versatile and like to explore other game engines (Godot Engine, Unreal Engine).

I also have a background in 3D graphics with Blender, which I have been using for over 10 years and teach at Montpellier 3 University in France.

I also like to make projects from start to end and practice modestly various softs like FL Studio and FMOD.

Fond of teamwork, I participate in a lot of gamejams to learn about other core video game professions, test and push my limits, always looking for technical solutions to produce games in a given time!
