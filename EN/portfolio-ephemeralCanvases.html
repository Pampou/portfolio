Ephemeral Canvases

  • 3D multiplayer contemplative experience
  • Made solo
  • [Unity & Mirror] - Development, Graphics, Sound Design & Game Design
  • Date: June 2022
  • Status: In Progress
Idea:

The game takes place in a loop of infinite sessions of a certain duration (15 minutes, more or less. Needs adjustment).

Let's say you join a session that has just started. You play a small, basic character who can move, jump, call (like in Journey). There are several of you wandering around in a kind of empty space for the moment. When suddenly, an orb of energy appears. You are free to appropriate it to obtain creative power (to be redefined perhaps). Once you have enough of this power, you can activate this power to create matter around you. And this matter can be rocks, trees, bushes, flowers, platforms... etc. Everything will be done in a semi-procedural way. Semi because you choose the place where this content will be generated: where you activated your power.

You continue within your playfield or "canvas". And with the others, who can also have power, you will gradually create a small world, a work, a painting.

However, time passes and the session is coming to an end. At a specific moment, the session goes into DECAY mode: You can no longer create and new people can no longer join the session. You experience, like a cutscene, the decline of your world in order to return to total nothingness. End of the session. You return to the main menu of the game.

Except that at this point, a new menu is available: Gallery. And this menu will allow you to consult all the works, the paintings, that you have created with others until now.
